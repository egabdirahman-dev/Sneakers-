Our Story Crafted by Nature

From the pristine pastures of New Zealand to the bustling streets of modern cities, discover how natural merino wool revolutionized urban footwear.

The WoolTech Journey

In 2018, a simple observation sparked a revolution: millions of people were wearing synthetic, uncomfortable shoes while nature had already perfected the ultimate comfort material. Merino wool, with its incredible properties, was waiting to be transformed into modern footwear.

We partnered with New Zealand sheep farmers, Italian textile artisans, and German engineers to create something unprecedented: sneakers that feel like your favorite sweater, perform like athletic gear, and look like high fashion.

Our Milestones

Key moments in our journey to revolutionize footwear

Science of Comfort

Merino wool's natural properties make it the perfect material for modern footwear

Temperature Regulation

Merino wool fibers naturally regulate temperature, keeping your feet comfortable in any climate. The unique crimp structure creates millions of tiny air pockets that insulate against both heat and cold.

Performance: 98%

Moisture Wicking

Each wool fiber can absorb up to 30% of its weight in moisture without feeling wet. This incredible capacity keeps your feet dry and prevents the growth of odor-causing bacteria.

Efficiency: 95%

Odor Resistant

Natural lanolin and antimicrobial properties prevent bacterial growth. Your shoes stay fresh longer, reducing the need for frequent washing and extending their lifespan.

Effectiveness: 92%
