# Wool Sneaker E-commerce Site - Design Style Guide

## Design Philosophy

### Core Concept: "Natural Wool Meets Urban Tech"
The design embodies the intersection of natural comfort and modern technology, creating a sophisticated e-commerce experience that feels both organic and cutting-edge. The aesthetic balances warmth and precision, reflecting the premium nature of merino wool footwear.

### Visual Language
- **Organic Minimalism**: Clean lines with subtle organic curves inspired by wool fiber structures
- **Tactile Sophistication**: Visual textures that evoke the softness and warmth of natural wool
- **Modern Craftsmanship**: Contemporary design that honors traditional textile artistry
- **Sustainable Luxury**: Premium feel without ostentation, emphasizing natural beauty

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors (Low Saturation)
- **Warm White**: #FEFCF8 (main background, represents clean wool)
- **Oat**: #E8DCC6 (secondary backgrounds, warm neutral)
- **Mist Grey**: #B8B2A7 (text, subtle contrasts)
- **Charcoal**: #4A453E (primary text, deep contrast)

### Accent Colors
- **Sage**: #A8B5A0 (subtle green, natural accent)
- **Warm Stone**: #D4C4B0 (interactive elements, gentle warmth)
- **Soft Taupe**: #C9B8A8 (borders, dividers)

### Functional Colors
- **Success**: #7A8471 (low-saturation green)
- **Warning**: #B8A082 (muted amber)
- **Error**: #A67C7C (soft red)

## Typography

### Primary Font: "Canela" (Display)
- Used for: Main headings, brand name, hero text
- Characteristics: Elegant serif with organic curves, premium feel
- Weights: Light, Regular, Medium

### Secondary Font: "Suisse Int'l" (Body)
- Used for: Body text, navigation, UI elements
- Characteristics: Clean sans-serif, excellent readability
- Weights: Regular, Medium, SemiBold

### Font Hierarchy
- **H1**: Canela Medium, 3.5rem (56px)
- **H2**: Canela Regular, 2.5rem (40px)
- **H3**: Canela Light, 1.875rem (30px)
- **Body**: Suisse Int'l Regular, 1rem (16px)
- **Small**: Suisse Int'l Regular, 0.875rem (14px)

## Visual Effects & Styling

### Background Effects
- **Primary**: Subtle wool-fiber inspired noise texture
- **Secondary**: Gentle gradient overlays using oat and mist-grey
- **Interactive**: Soft glow effects on hover states

### Animation Library Usage
- **Anime.js**: Smooth micro-interactions, button states, cart animations
- **Splitting.js**: Text reveal animations for brand story sections
- **Typed.js**: Typewriter effect for hero taglines
- **ECharts.js**: Subtle data visualizations for product features

### Hover Effects
- **Product Cards**: Gentle lift with soft shadow expansion
- **Buttons**: Color transition with subtle scale (1.02x)
- **Images**: Ken Burns effect with overlay text reveals
- **Navigation**: Underline animation with color transition

### Scroll Motion
- **Reveal Animations**: Elements fade in with 20px upward motion
- **Parallax**: Subtle background movement (max 8% translateY)
- **Stagger**: Sequential reveals for product grids
- **Timing**: 200ms duration with ease-out curve

## Layout & Spacing

### Grid System
- **Container**: Max-width 1200px, centered
- **Columns**: 12-column grid with 24px gutters
- **Breakpoints**: 
  - Mobile: 320px-768px
  - Tablet: 768px-1024px
  - Desktop: 1024px+

### Spacing Scale
- **XS**: 8px
- **SM**: 16px
- **MD**: 24px
- **LG**: 48px
- **XL**: 96px

## Component Styling

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Warm Stone background, Charcoal text, rounded corners
- **Secondary**: Transparent with Soft Taupe border
- **Hover**: Gentle color transition with scale effect

### Cards
- **Background**: Warm White with subtle shadow
- **Border**: 1px Soft Taupe
- **Hover**: Lift effect with expanded shadow

### Forms
- **Input Fields**: Oat background, Soft Taupe border
- **Focus**: Sage accent border
- **Labels**: Mist Grey text

### Navigation
- **Background**: Translucent Warm White
- **Links**: Charcoal with Sage hover
- **Active**: Underline with Sage color

## Imagery Style

### Product Photography
- **Lighting**: Soft, natural lighting
- **Background**: Clean white or subtle oat gradients
- **Composition**: Minimal, focus on texture and details
- **Color Grading**: Warm, low-contrast tones

### Lifestyle Photography
- **Setting**: Urban environments with natural elements
- **Mood**: Calm, sophisticated, aspirational
- **Color Palette**: Consistent with brand colors
- **Composition**: Generous negative space

### Hero Images
- **Style**: Abstract wool textures, fiber close-ups
- **Treatment**: Soft focus, artistic interpretation
- **Color**: Desaturated with warm undertones
- **Composition**: Vertical orientation for impact

## Interactive Elements

### Shopping Cart
- **Style**: Slide-out panel with Warm White background
- **Animation**: Smooth slide with backdrop blur
- **Items**: Clean list with product images
- **Total**: Prominent display with Sage accent

### Product Filters
- **Layout**: Horizontal bar with toggle buttons
- **Active State**: Sage background, Charcoal text
- **Hover**: Soft Taupe background

### Size Selection
- **Display**: Grid layout with rounded buttons
- **Available**: Oat background, Charcoal border
- **Selected**: Sage background, white text
- **Unavailable**: Mist Grey with strikethrough

This design system creates a cohesive, premium experience that honors both the natural origins of wool and the technological innovation of modern footwear design.
